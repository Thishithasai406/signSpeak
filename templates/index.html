About SignSpeak

To be honest, my journey with sign language began after watching a K-drama, Twinkling Watermelon. It might sound funny, but I was mesmerized by how so much emotion and meaning could be shared without a single word. That moment made me realize the incredible power of hand movements to tell stories, express feelings, and connect people.

This fascination grew into SignSpeak‚Äîmy way of combining technology with empathy. I wanted to create a space where learning American Sign Language (ASL) feels natural, engaging, and heartfelt. Through this project, I hope to bridge the gap between hearing and non-hearing communities, inspire curiosity, and give everyone the confidence to communicate through the universal language of gestures.
